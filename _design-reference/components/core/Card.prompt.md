General-purpose surface container. Hairline border, small radius, navy-tinted shadow.

```jsx
<Card interactive accent padding="lg">…</Card>
<Card variant="navy">…</Card>
```

`variant`: `surface` (white), `subtle` (off-white), `navy` (gradient, white text). `accent` adds a gold top rule. `interactive` lifts on hover. `padding`: `none`/`sm`/`md`/`lg`.
